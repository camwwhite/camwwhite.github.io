About Me

Characterizing galaxies in the early universe to constrain reionization

I am a graduate student at the University of Arizona working with Prof. Daniel Stark in studying the rest-ultraviolet spectra of the first galaxies in order to understand their contribution to Reionization. In 2019, I graduated with concurrent B.S. degrees in Physics and Astrophysics from Arizona State University where I worked with Prof. Rogier Windhorst searching for active galactic nuclei in the JWST North Ecliptic Pole Time-Domain Field.
